ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE:
TourNaija is a tech-based startup consisting of 6 members who realized the problem tourists encounter in locating tourists sites in Nigeria and even in other parts of the world, and are working hard to see that that problem is solved by providing a platform where tourists get to not only locate tourist sites, but also get necesary information about those sites. They (TourNaija) make tourism in Nigeria a fun experience.

OUR MOTTO:
Serving you better

OUR AIM
To serve as a platform that advertises Nigeria as a tourist center and provide necessary information to tourists.

OUR VISION
To become a world class advertising platform and make toursim a vital tool for enhancing the revenue base of Nigeria.

OUR MISSION
To help Tourists locate and enjoy tourist services that are available in the Nigeria.
